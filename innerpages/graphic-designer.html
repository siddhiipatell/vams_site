Benefits

Virtual Ads management system (VAMS) is the single platform for companies for managing and monitoring the operations of the Active Directory service that is mostly found in Windows Server operating system. It is used to easily launch, display ads, social media ads, and email that engages existing customers, attracts new customers, and grows revenue.

  • Anyone can be a member of it
  • Customer can put a request for Ads
  • Publisher can earn money by publishing Ads

We aim to make one Virtual Ads Management System that is compatible with any media such as outdoor, digital media, and print media. Does not require individual systems for individual media. This system will work for any media. It’s very cost-effective.
